Key Business Metrics

Gross Sales = Qty × Unit Price
Net Sales = Gross − Discount
COGS = Qty × Unit Cost
Gross Profit = Net Sales − COGS
Margin % = Gross Profit / Net Sales × 100
Total = Net Sales + Tax (8%)
AOV = SUM(Total) / COUNT(Orders)
Return Rate = Return Qty / Qty × 100
